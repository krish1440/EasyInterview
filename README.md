# **EasyInterview – Your Personal AI Interview Coach**

**EasyInterview** is an AI-powered mock interview platform built to deliver a realistic, interactive training experience for job seekers. Developed with **React** and powered by **Google’s Gemini 2.5 Multimodal AI**, it simulates real-world interview environments through voice interaction, video analysis, and personalized feedback using an intelligent AI recruiter named **“Ava.”**

---

## **Key Capabilities**

### **Real-time Voice Interview System**
- Enables continuous, hands-free interview conversations without typing  
- Uses advanced speech recognition to capture spoken responses with high accuracy  
- Employs natural **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** for fluid, human-like AI interactions  
- Replicates real interview pacing, tone, and question flow

### **Multimodal Visual Intelligence**
- Activates webcam-based candidate observation and analysis  
- Tracks behavioral indicators including:
  - Eye contact focus  
  - Sitting posture and body alignment  
  - Facial expressions and confidence signals  
- Delivers on-screen coaching tips in real time to enhance communication presence  

### **Resume & Career Context Engine**
- Allows uploading of:
  - Resume/CV documents  
  - Target job descriptions  
- Applies AI content analysis to:
  - Generate role-specific technical questions  
  - Create behavioral situation and leadership scenarios  
  - Dynamically adapt questions based on interview performance  
- Maintains a fully personalized interview flow aligned with career objectives  

---

## **Interview Performance Analytics Dashboard**

### **Performance Radar**
- Visual proficiency scoring across critical interview skills:
  - Communication clarity  
  - Domain & technical expertise  
  - Logical problem solving  
  - Confidence & articulation  
  - Body language effectiveness  

### **Question-level Feedback Engine**
- Provides detailed evaluation for every interview answer:
  - Strength analysis  
  - Missed improvement opportunities  
  - AI-generated best-response examples for comparison  

### **Resume Health Diagnostic**
- Calculates **ATS compatibility score** based on job role requirements  
- Performs deep keyword gap analysis to:
  - Highlight missing role-specific skills  
  - Suggest resume optimization improvements  

### **Personalized Learning Roadmap**
- Builds a growth-focused action plan containing:
  - Skill enhancement exercises  
  - Interview practice recommendations  
  - Curated learning links and preparation resources

## **Interactive User Experience**
